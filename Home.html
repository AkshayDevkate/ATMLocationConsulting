About Essen City

Essen is the central and second largest city of the Ruhr, the largest urban area in Germany. With poipulation of 583,109 makes it the tenth largest city in germany and forth largest city of federal state of North Rhine Westphalia.

Essen comprises fifty boroughs(a town or district which is an administrative unit) which turn are grouped into nine suburban districts called Stadtbezirke often named aftermost important boroughs
